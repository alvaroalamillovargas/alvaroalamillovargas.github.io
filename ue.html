Unreal Engine Quizz Game

For this project I had to make a Quizz Game, I decided to use the oportunity to learn about hit registration, health systems, particles and cutscenes.

I did it in Unreal Engine 4 using C++ and Blueprint. The enemy models are from the Unreal asset store, the rest of assets like the torches or the doors I did on 3DS Max
